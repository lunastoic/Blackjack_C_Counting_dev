# Privacy Policy — Blackjack Card Counter

**Effective date:** July 17, 2026

Blackjack Card Counter ("the app") is a blackjack strategy and card-counting
training app. It is a simulation only: no real-money gambling, no wagering, and
no connection to any casino or betting service.

## Data we collect

**None.** The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

- No account or sign-up is required.
- All game progress, statistics, achievements, and settings are stored locally
  on your device only and never leave it.
- The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no
  third-party SDKs that collect data.
- The app does not require internet access to function.

## Data deletion

Deleting the app from your device permanently deletes all of its data.

## Children

The app is rated for adult audiences due to simulated gambling content and is
not directed at children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future update adds online
features), the updated policy will be posted at this same location before the
change takes effect.
